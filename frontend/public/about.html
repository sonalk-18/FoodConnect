About FoodConnect

Bridging the gap between surplus food and those who need it most.

Our Mission

At FoodConnect, we aim to reduce food waste and combat hunger. By connecting households, restaurants, and businesses with NGOs and community kitchens, we ensure surplus food is never wasted and reaches people in need.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no edible food goes to waste, and every community has access to nutritious meals. We strive to create a connected ecosystem of donors, volunteers, and organizations working together for a hunger-free society.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Caring for the community and those in need.
  • Sustainability: Reducing food waste and promoting eco-friendly practices.
  • Collaboration: Building a network of donors, volunteers, and NGOs.
  • Efficiency: Ensuring food reaches beneficiaries quickly and safely.

Gaming with FoodConnect

Play. Earn. Save. — Bringing fun and purpose together.

Why Gaming?

FoodConnect isn’t just for NGOs and donors — it’s also for you! Our gaming feature allows regular users to earn exciting rewards and discount coupons simply by playing games within the app.

What You Get

  • Earn rewards and coupons by playing fun mini-games.
  • Get discounts at partnered restaurants and cafes.
  • Buy surplus fresh food from restaurants and café owners at affordable prices.

With FoodConnect Games, saving food and enjoying fresh meals has never been this fun!
