Donate Your Surplus Food

Every meal counts! Help us feed the hungry and reduce food waste.

Who Can Donate?

Restaurants, households, cafes, caterers, events, and food businesses – anyone with surplus food can donate through FoodConnect.

How It Works

  1. Register: Sign up as a donor on FoodConnect.
  2. List Food: Add details about your surplus food – type, quantity, and pickup time.
  3. NGO Matching: Our system connects your donation to a nearby NGO or community kitchen.
  4. Pickup & Delivery: Volunteers collect and deliver the food safely.

Benefits of Donating

  • Make a positive impact on your community.
  • Reduce food waste and contribute to sustainability.
  • Help nourish people who need it most.
